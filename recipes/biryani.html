Description

This biryani recipe is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, meat (chicken, beef, or lamb), and a blend of spices. It's a one-pot meal that's perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 pound meat (chicken, beef, or lamb), cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and mint leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Wash the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add the meat pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes until the tomatoes soften.
  5. Add the soaked and drained rice to the pot, followed by the water. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  7. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 10 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and mint leaves.
