Description

This sandwich recipe is a quick and easy meal option, perfect for lunch or a light dinner. You can customize it with your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 cup lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Layer the lettuce, tomato, and cucumber on one slice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Top with the other slice of bread, mayonnaise side down.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half and serve.
