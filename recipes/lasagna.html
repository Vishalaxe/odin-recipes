Welcome to my lasagna recipes page

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It's baked until bubbly and golden.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  5. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, then half of the ricotta mixture, followed by a third of the mozzarella cheese and a third of the meat.
  6. Repeat the layers, ending with noodles, sauce, and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
